We want to make your experience with the rgOne apps as pleasant and productive as possible. [Please email or call us](#redpoint-contact-information) when you need help.

## Troubleshooting information to have on hand when you contact us

When you [contact us about a problem](#redpoint-contact-information), please include the following information:

- Product version number (displayed on the lower right-hand corner of the rgOne logon page).
- Your:
    - Name
    - Company name
    - Telephone number
    - Email address
- Operating system and version number.
- Exact wording of any error messages that appear.
- Exactly what you were doing when the problem or error occurred.
- How you tried to resolve the problem.

We may also ask you for:
- A sample of the input data.
- Your system configuration.
